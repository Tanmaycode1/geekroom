About Hack Splash

Welcome to Hack Splash, a thrilling 36-hour hackathon hosted at JIMS Rohini Sector-5, a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, in February 2024. More than just a competition, it's an immersive experience filled with innovation, exciting prizes, swags, and a lot of fun. It's your opportunity to connect with experienced mentors and judges.

Organized by Geek Room, a vibrant community dedicated to enhancing coding skills, started as an open community for solving tech-related queries and participating in college competitions. In a short span, it has grown to include 6000+ participants from colleges across India. Now, launching chapters in different colleges, we're excited to have it at JIMS!

Join us on this exciting coding journey and become a part of Geek Room's JIMS Chapter. Connect with us on Geek Room's JIMS Chapter.

Problem Statements

Explore the Problem Statements

You are free to work on any problem, but there's a special prize for the team with the best project based on our designated problem statements below.

Problem Statement Category Description
Adaptive Learning Pathways Ed-Tech Develop a platform that leverages advanced AI algorithms to dynamically adapt and personalize learning pathways for students based on their individual learning styles, preferences, and performance data. The solution should enhance engagement, comprehension, and retention, catering to diverse educational needs.
Transforming Emergency Healthcare Orchestration Health-Tech Develop innovative technological solutions to revolutionize emergency response systems, aiming for unprecedented levels of efficiency. Address not only reduced response times but also intricate aspects such as predictive analytics, real-time patient data sharing, and groundbreaking communication protocols between emergency services and healthcare facilities. The challenge is to create a seamlessly orchestrated ecosystem that not only expedites response but transforms how resources are allocated, conserving and optimizing them for an unparalleled impact on the healthcare system's efficiency.
Intelligent Traffic Management Smart Cities Design a comprehensive system to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and enhance road safety in urban areas. The solution should include real-time traffic monitoring, predictive analytics, and smart traffic signal control to create a more efficient and adaptive transportation network.
Revolutionizing UPI for Seamless Transactions Fin-Tech Reimagine the UPI system to elevate user experience and transaction efficiency. Develop innovative solutions that go beyond the current framework, focusing on aspects such as speed, scalability, and user-friendly interfaces. Your challenge is to bring fresh perspectives to UPI, optimizing its capabilities and exploring novel features that enhance the overall transactional ecosystem. Whether it's streamlining processes, introducing unique functionalities, or improving interoperability, your goal is to contribute to the evolution of UPI for a more intuitive and efficient digital payment landscape.
Autonomous Satellite Repair Space Exploration and Technology Design an autonomous robotic system capable of repairing and maintaining satellites in Earth's orbit. The solution should address challenges such as navigation, manipulation in microgravity, and real-time decision-making to extend the operational life of satellites and reduce space debris.

F.A.Q

  • What is Hack Splash and what makes it unique?

    Hack Splash is a groundbreaking hackathon jointly organized by JIMS Sector-5 Rohini and Geek Room. It provides a unique experience, redefining the boundaries of creativity and technology, transcending conventional hackathons.

  • When and where is Code Kshetra 2024 taking place?

    Hack Splash 2024 is an offline event scheduled for 5th-6th February. The venue is JIMS Sector-5 Rohini, near Rithala Metro Station . It's a 36-hour hackathon that promises an immersive experience in tech innovation.

  • What is the prize pool for Hack Splash, and how are the prizes distributed?

    Hack Splash boasts a prize pool worth INR 50,00,000+, with a cash prize worth INR 25,000+. Details about the prize distribution will be announced closer to the event.

  • What engaging activities can participants expect during Hack Splash?

    Hack Splash features live project presentations, idea pitching, judging by esteemed panels, and a fun and games round with prizes to keep participants engaged throughout the hackathon.

  • What exclusive perks are provided to participants?

    Participants at Hack Splash enjoy exclusive perks, including free swags, networking opportunities with industry experts, goodies, accommodation, meals, and much more, enhancing their overall experience.

  • How does Hack Splash encourage innovation and problem-solving?

    Hack Splash challenges participants to push their boundaries and foster inventive solutions to real-world issues. It celebrates the union of innovation and technology, providing a platform for participants to showcase their skills.

  • Who are the organizers of Hack Splash, and what is their mission?

    Hack Splash is hosted by the collaborative efforts of JIMS Sector-5 Rohini and Geek Room. Their mission is to provide a platform for tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills and amplify their potential through an exciting blend of competition, learning, and groundbreaking innovation.

  • How can participants connect with like-minded individuals and industry experts during Hack Splash?

    Participants can connect with like-minded individuals and industry experts through networking sessions, project collaborations, and various interactive opportunities provided during Hack Splash. It's a unique chance to learn, compete, and establish valuable connections.

    Yes, participants are required to follow the code of conduct outlined at [Code of Conduct]. It ensures a positive and respectful environment for all attendees.

    To register for Hack Splash 2024, visit the official website and follow the registration instructions. The registration link on Devfolio can be found [here]. Complete the registration form and make sure to adhere to the specified deadlines.
