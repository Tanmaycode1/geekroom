About TechMinds

Welcome to TechMinds, a thrilling 30-hour hackathon hosted at IITM Janakpuri , a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, in March 2024. More than just a competition, it's an immersive experience filled with innovation, exciting prizes, swags, and a lot of fun. It's your opportunity to connect with experienced mentors and judges.

Organized by Geek Room, a vibrant community dedicated to enhancing coding skills, started as an open community for solving tech-related queries and participating in college competitions. In a short span, it has grown to include 6000+ participants from colleges across India. Now, launching chapters in different colleges, we're excited to have it at IITM!

Join us on this exciting coding journey and become a part of Geek Room's IITM Chapter. Connect with us on Geek Room's IITM Chapter.

F.A.Q

  • Who can participate in TechMinds Hackathon 2024?

    TechMinds Hackathon 2024 is open to all students from any college.

  • How many members can be in a team?

    Teams can have 1 to4 members .

  • Do participants need to have coding experience?

    No, TechMinds is designed for everyone, from tech enthusiasts to those taking their first steps in the digital world.

  • What are the themes for the projects?

    Themes include Machine Learning, Smart Automation, Med Tech, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Disaster Management, Smart Education, Smart Agriculture, AI Assistant, Gamification, and IoT. Participants can also choose their own theme for their project.

  • What are the rules regarding projects?

    Projects must address real-life problems or contribute to a social cause to be considered for acceptance.

  • What are the judging criteria?

    Criteria may include innovation, technical implementation, user experience, scalability, and alignment with the theme. The judges' decision will be considered final.

  • What are the event dates and duration?

    The event will take place from 9:30 AM on March 15th to 4:30 PM on March 16th, 2024.
